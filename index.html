    About / how this works

    Loads the schedule from DaySmart Recreation's public JSON:API (no login, no scraping). Stores in your browser's localStorage. Detects four issue classes and lets you simulate single-swap fixes that improve fairness.

    Issue weights (penalty function): game-count variance ×4, home/away imbalance ×2, slot-distribution variance ×1, same-matchup back-to-back ×8.

    Caveats: A date-swap can't fix uneven game counts or home/away imbalance — those need a matchup change. The swap engine still suggests fixes for slot fairness and back-to-back issues.
